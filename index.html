Our Story

Small Farm,
Big Heart

Eli started with just six hens and a dream. Over a decade later, our flock has grown — but our values haven't. Every egg is collected by hand each morning, washed gently, and ready for your family before noon.

Our hens roam freely on open pasture, eating natural grasses, insects, and non-GMO supplemental feed. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just real eggs the way nature intended.

The Process

From Our Hens to
Your Hands

We keep it simple because nature keeps it simple. No middleman, no cold storage, no mystery.

🌅
Dawn Collection

Eli walks the coops at sunrise, hand-collecting each egg from the nesting boxes while the hens are out in the pasture.

🚿
Gentle Washing

Each egg is dry-brushed and lightly rinsed with warm water. No chemicals, no bleach — we keep the natural bloom intact when possible.

📦
Packed Fresh

Sorted by size and packed into reusable cartons by 9am — marked with the date laid so you always know what you're getting.

🛒
Ready by Noon

Pick up from our roadside stand or front-porch honesty box, seven days a week. Truly farm-to-door.
